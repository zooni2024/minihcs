Miniature Cows

Miniature Breed: (Mutated and Developed by Dr.KRISHNAM RAJU)
Height: Typically stands below 2.5 feet.
Varieties: •Ground Surface •Hard Surface •Smooth Surface 
Nature: Miniature cows are known for their gentle and friendly nature, making them suitable companions both indoors and in outdoor environments.
Special Qualities: Valued for their compact size and versatility, these cows are a unique addition to homes or farms Like Pets.
Exclusivity: Miniature cows are exclusively available at Nadipathy Goshala. You won’t find them anywhere else in the world.
Among the Smallest Breeds in the World: The Miniature breed ranks among the world’s smallest cows, offering a rare and distinctive option for those seeking something extraordinary.
Milk Production: Each cow produces approximately 1 to 3 liters of milk daily.
Milk Fat: 6 to 10% and High in Medical Properties.                                    
Color Varieties: Black, White, Brown and Multi color.
Lifespan: Miniature cows have a lifespan of around 20 years.
