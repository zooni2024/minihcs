Meet Our Farm Friends

  • Stella

    Kind, cuddly, and extremely friendly cow from a mini silver galloway with panda markings and BCC The Famous Silver Splendor is the sire.

  • Millie

    Beautiful dun miniature highland cow with a sassy, sweet, and independent personality!

  • Vivian

    Miniature Female Horse with a love for hay and relaxing! And the prettiest hair on the farm!

  • Maverick

    They call him Maverick for good reason, this miniature male horse loves to run!! When hes not running, hes either eating or hanging out with Vivian!

  • Midnight

    Midnight is our mid-Size Highland Heifer who is the queen of the pasture!

  • Raven

    Mini highland heifer

  • Poppy

    This red highland female is 3 years old and as sweet as they come!

  • Petunia

    Shes DUN! This highland girl likes her space and lots of love!

  • Titan

    Our first Highpark micro-bull arrived March 1st 2025! Titan lives up to his name with a mighty presence and a heart of gold. His shaggy coat, curious eyes, and calm confidence, are already stealing hearts and leading the way through our acres!

  • Pumpkin

    This sweet baby is a yellow miniature highland calf and was born June 2nd 2025! She loves snuggling and sitting in the leaves under the Autumn trees!

  • Brutus

    Check out our Highland Sire Brutus!

  • Chickens

    With over 25 various breeds of chickens, we pick fresh eggs every day and will be selling them this upcoming spring! Penny, Pearl, Poppy, Lucky, Butterball, Fancy, Big Daddy, Rosie, Lily, Shadow, CoCo, Black Betty, Blue Bell, Buttons, Simba, Jersey, Pixie, and Goldie

  • Ducks

    It all started with Daisy!! In April of 2024 she arrived! When our sons friends needed to find the little duck a home, we took her in! We couldnt resist getting the little duck a friend! Then there was two! We fell in love with them! Our farm continued to grow after that and is still growing!

  • Tux

    Tux is a Valais Blacknose lamb who has no regard for your personal space! He will come sit right in your lap and steal all your attention!

  • Tango

    Tango is a Valais Blacknose lamb. He was born a triplet, he is calm and sweet and loves to be anywhere next to Tux.
