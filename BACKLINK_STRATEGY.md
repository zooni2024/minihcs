## 1. Top Platforms for Manual Backlinks (FREE)
Create profiles and post helpful content on these platforms to get initial backlinks.

| Platform | Type | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pinterest** | Social | Create "Pins" for your articles (e.g., "Mini Highland Cow Care"). Link back to your article. |
| **Quora** | Q&A | Answer questions about animal care. Include a link to your detailed article as a source. |
| **Reddit** | Community | Join subreddits like `r/animals`, `r/farming`, `r/pets`. Share helpful advice with a link to your site (avoid spamming). |
| **Medium** | Content | Re-publish short versions of your articles and link to the full version on Mini HCS. |
| **YouTube** | Video | Make 30-second shorts of animals and put your link in the description. |

## 4. Broken Link Building
1. Find old animal articles on other sites that have broken links using tools like "Ahrefs Broken Link Checker" (free version).
2. Email the site owner: *"Hey, this link is broken, but I have a high-quality guide on the same topic at Mini HCS that you could use as a replacement."*

## 5. Infographics & Linkable Assets
